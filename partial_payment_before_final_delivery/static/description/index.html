Payment Terms in ODOO

Payments plays an important role in any business , which shows the collection capability of any business and the future of the business.

In the following, User will come to know about the features and configurations of Payment terms created. In ODOO we can configure different payment terms depending upon our relation with customers and vendors and capability of customers to pay the amounts in the specific time period.

Receive partial payment before delivery

Features


Delivery Order will not be validated unless certain amount of payment is received from the customer as defined in the payment terms.


You can set any percentage of payment required to be received from the customer on payment terms before a delivery.

Configuration

For configuring, the receipt of payment before delivery to the customer, you will go to the payment terms in the configuration tab, under the accounting module.


On the payment terms, You will see a new check box with name “Partial Payment Term”. When you select this check box and add percentage value in the terms, the system will not allow delivering the products unless this amount is received from the customer or you can also select a fixed amount instead of percentage.
